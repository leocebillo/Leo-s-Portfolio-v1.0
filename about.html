05 / about

Who I am.

I'm Leo — an 18-year-old photographer, musician, and developer based in Texas. Three different crafts, but they all come from the same place: I like making things, seeing them through, and getting a little better each time I pick the camera, the guitar, or the keyboard back up.

how I got here

Three stories, three crafts.

Photography

I started off taking decent photos with my phone and received positive feedback, which encouraged me to try more when taking pictures even getting into editing them. Eventually, I invested in a Nikon D7500 and a Nikkor 55–300mm lens to take my photography to the next level.

Music

I originally learned how to play guitar from my Elementary school music teacher as she had a Guitar Club. From that day on, I knew I loved music and I learned over time how to express myself through lyrics and slowly learned how to produce music on my own.

Code

Usually, I have a lot of time on my hands and I decided to learn how to make a simple website for myself. After realizing I learned quickly and was getting the hang of it, I decided to continue pursuing coding.
